Project

Creating sound for animated movie Insignitus

This project emphasize animation and sound. In the field of animation all the credits go to Ema Matica who has envisioned visual part of the project. An idea was born from a wish to convey a message and to answer a problematic question whether a violence is hereditary trait or learnt behavior or personal choice.

Sound has been given an important role in this joint venture.Moreover, it has been decided that visual part of the project will look like kid's drawing and it won't depict any form of violence, instead the sound will provide us with that. The focus is on how to show with the sound what is not shown with a picture, that is how to tell a story when there is no picture. The whole process of this project has required a lot of investigating, recording of different sounds, as well as experimenting with them, in order to achieve a sound that will arouse certain emotions such as happiness and relaxation in the nature or sorrow and uneasiness because of the violence.
