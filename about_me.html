Something about me...

My name is Valentina Špoljarić. I am a student at the University North in the department of Media design in Koprivnica (Croatia). I've specified in graphic design, sound, photography, animation and video editing. I worked on promotional materials such as flyers, business cards, posters, catalogs and similar.

My experience of one year as a radio host has helped me to develop a better understanding of the sound and because of that my biggest passion is to work with anything related to the sound, that is, to record and create sound and music, as well as editing it. I was also a radio editor, so I am familiar with the whole process of creating and editing radio broadcast. I have also worked on synchronization of the sound for an animated movie.
